## Short post

Everyone argues about what a card is worth. The question underneath that is simpler: how badly do players actually want it right now?

So I built a model that scores every Magic card 0 to 100 on demand, which is buy-side desire to own it today. It leans on EDHREC (how many Commander decks run the card, the closest thing to a real demand meter), plus whether the price has been quietly climbing, whether the card is legal where people play, and how often Wizards has had to reprint it to keep up.

The results match the gut check. The most-wanted cards are the tutors, fast mana, and value engines that go into deck after deck. And there is a fun twist: demand is not the same as price. A 30 cent staple that everyone runs can be in higher demand than a pricey card almost nobody plays.

Demand is also the catalyst that drives the moves my other models chase. Supply going down (a reprint) plus demand going up is what causes most big price spikes.

What card do you think is the single most wanted in Commander right now? 👀

## Posting tips

- Lead with the question (how wanted is it), not the math.
- Frame it as a fun tool, not financial advice.
- Have the demand-is-not-price twist ready for the comments (cheap but ubiquitous vs pricey but unplayed).
- Check the group's self-promo rules first.
